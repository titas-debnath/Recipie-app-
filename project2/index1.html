This simple dish transforms a pantry staple into a flavourful meal in minutes, perfect for a quick lunch, late night snack, or a comforting bite anytime.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons of oil (vegetable or olive)

    • Optional:

  • Chilli flakes, to taste
  • Soy sauce
  • Spring onions, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:

    Prepare the Garlic:

  • Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the chopped garlic and saut√© until it turns golden brown and fragrant.
  • If you are using chili flakes, add them with the garlic.

    • Cook the noodles:

  • Add the noodles into the boiling water.
  • Drain most of the water from the cooked noodles, leaving a little moisture.

    • Combine:

  • Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the garlic and the seasoning packet.
  • Toss the noodles well to coat them with the garlic oil.
  • If you are going to add soy sauce, add it now, and toss the noodles.

    • Serve:

  • Transfer the Garlic Maggi to a serving bowl.
  • Garnish with chopped spring onions, if desired.
  • Serve hot.
